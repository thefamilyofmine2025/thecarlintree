7. Joseph CARLIN [scrapbook] (Matthias , Peter ) was born on 31 Mar 1806 in SHELBY CO KY. He died on 30 Mar 1874 in DAVIESS COUNTY CO.. He was buried in Rosehill Elmwood Cemetery.

Joseph entered into a marriage contract on 4 Jul 1833 1 in SPENCER CO KY. He was counted in a census 2 in 1850 in Daviess County, District 1. He was counted in a census 3 in 1860 in Davies County Kentucky. He was counted in a census 4 in 1870 in Daviess County KY.

Engraver made an error; the birth date should be March 31, 1806, not 1816. The stones are no longer upright but are embedded into the ground, you have to be right upon them to see them. These are in the area of Rosehill that was at the time the Carlin Family Farm. These are the only stones left that I have been able to find but I understand several other members may be buried around them. I did take a rod and stick in the ground 6-8 inches for several feet around them and found nothing but they are somewhat isolated from other burials with stones.

Don Carlin
