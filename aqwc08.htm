2Evansville Courier. "PETERSBURG, Ind. ? James Edward Melhiser Jr., 56, went home to be with the Lord on Sunday, Feb. 17, 2008, at 7 a.m. at his home in Alford.

He was born in Owensboro, Ky., on Dec. 9, 1951. He was the son of the late James Edward Melhiser Sr. and his surviving mother, Frances Magnetta "Maggie" (Bellew) Melhiser.

He graduated from Petersburg High School in 1969. On Dec. 1, 1972, he married Amy Darlene (Hawkins) Melhiser, and they spent 35 years together in marriage. He worked 36 years at Texas Gas Transmission Corp., where in Dec. 2006, he retired. His passion in life was woodworking for the family business. He enjoyed remodeling his home and spending valuable time with his family. He loved to study the word of God, along with Jewish culture and religion. He was a member of Antioch Christian Church in Washington, Ind.

Surviving are his wife, Amy (Hawkins) Melhiser; his three daughters, Dana (Melhiser) Wahl of Huntingburg, Erin (Melhiser) and husband, Brian Willis, of Petersburg and Krissy (Melhiser) and husband, Ryan Burkhart, of Colorado; three grandchildren, Lily and Kennedy Willis and Colton Kirk; three brothers, David (Shirley) Melhiser, John (Theresa) Melhiser and Joe Melhiser; 20 nieces and nephews; and 12 great-nieces and nephews.

The funeral services will be held at Harris Funeral Home in Petersburg, Ind. The viewing was Tuesday. Funeral services will be 1 p.m. today, Feb. 20, 2008. He will be laid to rest in Walnut Hills Cemetery in Petersburg, Ind. Officiating the service is Pastor Dan Koenig of Indianapolis.".
